HOW IT WORKS

MediMind analyzes your selected symptoms and compares them against a structured medical dataset. The system identifies patterns and matches them with possible conditions, giving you a quick overview of what your symptoms may indicate. It is designed to be fast, intuitive, and accessible for everyday use.

1. ENTER SYMPTOMS

Search and select symptoms using the intelligent suggestion system.

2. DATA MATCHING

The backend compares your symptoms with stored medical data.

3. GET RESULTS

Receive possible conditions ranked by match percentage.
